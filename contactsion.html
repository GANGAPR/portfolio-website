Project Overview

Contactsion is a mobile application designed to simplify contact management by allowing users to store contact details such as names, email addresses, and phone numbers by scanning business cards.

Problem Statement

  • The manual entry and management of business card information are time-consuming and prone to errors.
  • Professionals attending networking events or receiving multiple business cards find it challenging to keep track of contacts and often misplace physical cards.
  • Professionals who rely on paper-based business cards have limited search and organization capabilities, making it challenging to find specific contacts when needed.
  • Professionals frequently find themselves in situations where they urgently need to access a contact's information but are unable to do so accessibility.
  • Professionals often need to add specific details or notes to business card contacts, but paper-based cards do not provide a space for such annotations.

Solution

  • Develop an app to scan and digitize business card information accurately. Implement a user-friendly interface for efficient contact management.
  • App that allows users to scan and store business card details digitally, eliminating the need for physical cards, ensuring they are accessible.
  • Create app with contact management features, including search functionality, custom fields, and categorization options for easy access.
  • Develop an app to provide instant access to scanned contact details, ensuring that users can quickly retrieve and share contact information when needed.
  • Create app with customizable fields, enabling users to add additional information and notes to their digitized business card contacts.

Goal

To create a Card Scanner App that transforms the way professionals and businesses manage, organize, and access business card contact information, addressing the limitations of traditional paper-based card management methods.

Objectives

To develop accurate scanning technology, design an intuitive interface, implement cloud integration, support various card formats, enable user customization, prioritize data security, drive user adoption, commit to continuous improvement.

Conclusion

  • The app has significantly improved efficiency by eliminating the manual entry of adding contact information from paper business cards.
  • Users can access and manage their contacts seamlessly through the digital address book provided by the app, making it easy to categorize and search contacts.
  • Users can customize their contact entries with additional information and notes, enhancing the app's ability to cater to specific user preferences and needs.
  • Robust security measures safeguard users' contact information, preserving privacy and instilling confidence in the app's trustworthiness.
